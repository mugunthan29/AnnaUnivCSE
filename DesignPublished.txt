(57) Abstract :
A wall fixing device, comprises of a telescopic handle 2 with a soft padding for a user to acquire a grip onto, a container 6 attached to the rectangular plate 1 for storing
a putty material and a robotic arm attached to the rectangular plate 1 having an applying plate 1 at its end for applying putty material on the wall from the container 6, a
robotic link 3 attached to the rectangular plate 1, an artificial intelligence-based imaging unit 4 to determine if a wall surface is flat or a corner, a sensing module, an
ultrasonic sensor for detecting the unwanted material and the damage on the wall surface, and a microphone 5 for receiving audio commands from the user to trigger the
microcontroller.
